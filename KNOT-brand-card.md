# KNOT — Brand Card

## What it is
A platform that connects local businesses with content creators for real, trackable collaborations. Not influencer marketing — real partnerships.

## The name
**KNOT** — the knot that ties two sides together. Simple, short, memorable.

## Vibe (one sentence)
A space that feels like your favorite cafe — not a software product.

## Who uses it
- **Creators** — local micro-influencers, content creators (not mega-influencers)
- **Businesses** — boutique restaurants, spas, local shops

## Tone
Warm, grownup, editorial. Not corporate. Not wellness-app precious. Not influencer-platform flashy.

## Colors (current, to refine)
- Base: #EDE8E2 warm cream
- Primary green: #6B8F71
- Teal accent: #4ECDC4
- Terracotta: #CB997E
- Sand: #DDBEA9
- Dark text: #3D3229

## Typography (current)
- Headings: Lora (serif)
- Body: Quicksand (sans-serif)

Suggested upgrade: Canela Deck + Söhne

## Logo
**NO EXISTING LOGO — please design one.**

Concept direction:
- The "knot" = the tie that binds creator and business together
- Two loops/arcs that interlock, forming a knot shape
- OR abstract: two shapes that touch/connect
- Should work at 32px (favicon) and scale up
- Monochromatic + wordmark versions needed
- Wordmark: "KNOT" in a confident serif — NOT decorative, NOT flashy
- Should feel: grownup, warm, like a small studio not a tech startup

Avoid:
- Actual rope/knot illustrations (too literal)
- Heart shapes (too wedding)
- Abstract blobs (generic)
- All-caps sans wordmarks (too SaaS)

## References to match
- Aimé Leon Dore (warm editorial minimalism)
- Ghia (sophisticated pops)
- Poolsuite FM (retro warmth + modern UI)

## References to AVOID
- Airbnb (too corporate)
- Glossier (too young/millennial pink)
- SaaS dashboards
- Influencer platforms (Collabstr, Aspire)
- Wellness apps

## Key screens
1. Landing — first impression
2. Explore — discovery feed for creators
3. Knot detail — the wow moment (includes chat + proof + timeline)
4. Profile — trust score + stats

## What v1 got wrong
- Colors feel tired
- Empty states are generic gray
- Dashboards are text-only, no visual energy
- Auth pages feel corporate
- Missing "wow" moments (celebrations, animations)

## The feeling we want
"Finally, a space built for real connections — warm, trustworthy, and mine."
